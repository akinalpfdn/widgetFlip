# Privacy Policy for widgetFlip

**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## Overview

**widgetFlip** is a coin flipper application for iOS that allows you to flip a virtual coin and view your flip history. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

## Data Collection

**widgetFlip does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

### No Data We Collect
- No personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- No device identifiers or advertising identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
- No location data
- No usage analytics or crash reports
- No third-party analytics or tracking services

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following information **locally on your device only** using Apple's UserDefaults framework:

| Data | Purpose | Location |
|------|---------|----------|
| Current coin side (HEADS/TAILS) | Display in widget | Local App Group storage |
| Coin icon name | Display in widget | Local App Group storage |
| Last flip timestamp | Display in widget | Local App Group storage |
| Flip counter | Animation state | Local App Group storage |
| Flip history (max 50 entries) | Display recent flips | Local App Group storage |

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Is not synchronized across devices
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## App Group Usage

widgetFlip uses Apple's App Group capability (`group.com.akinalpfdn.widgetflip`) solely to share data between:
- The main app
- The home screen widget
- The lock screen widget/control center

This enables the widget to display your latest coin flip result. No data is transmitted outside your device.

## Network Activity

**widgetFlip does not make any network requests.** The app works entirely offline and requires no internet connection.

## Third-Party Services

**widgetFlip does not use any third-party services**, including but not limited to:
- Analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

Since we do not collect data, this privacy policy is unlikely to change. If we ever add features that collect data, we will update this policy and notify you.
